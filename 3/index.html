Pizza margherita recipe

This is a step by step guide to making a delicious pizza margherita.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, water, yeast, and salt. Knead until smooth and let it rise for 1 hour.
  2. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  5. Add slices of fresh mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven, let cool slightly, then slice and serve.
